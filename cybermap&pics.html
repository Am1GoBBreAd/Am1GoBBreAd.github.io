  • This is the full playable area of the Cyberpunk 2077 map. There are six city districts which we'll get to shortly, as well as the Badlands and outlying areas. The blue lines are roads which gives a good idea of where you can actually go in the game.

  • Each area is self-sufficient in that you'll find a selection of all the vendors, services, fast travel points and so on that you need. So you can easily just camp out in an area for a while to explore and see what's there without having to worry about needing to go anywhere else for supplies.

Each of these main districts is composed of additional sub districts and, again, can change in appearance as you move between them.

Fast travel points appear all over the map once you've found them meaning you can easily move between any area you've been to already.

  1. Watson - Northside, Arasaka Waterfront, Little China
  2. Westbrook - Japantown, North Oak, Charter Hill
  3. Corpo Plaza, Downtown
  4. Heywood - Wellsprings, The Glen
  5. Santo Domingo - Arroyo, Rancho Coranado
  6. Pacifica - Coastview, West Wind Estate

  • Drop Points (White)
  • Fast Travel (Blue)
